# Hypothesis Log

> **What this is**: A scratchpad for investment ideas between War Room sessions. Jot down anything — a news headline, a friend's tip, a shower thought. Write it however you want. The orchestrator will formalise it at the next session.
>
> **How to use**: Add a line under "Open Ideas" whenever something catches your eye. Plain language is fine — "RAM stocks running low, could that mean rising chip prices?" is a perfectly good entry. The Signal Generator will investigate it with real data at the next War Room.
>
> **What the orchestrator does at session start**: Reads this log, formalises each idea into a hypothesis (thesis type, testable prediction, invalidation criteria), and feeds it to the Signal Generator alongside their own signal search. Ideas that get investigated are moved to "Resolved" with the outcome.
